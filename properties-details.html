Building overview

Welcome to University Digs, the newest and most exciting apartment complex in Pittsburg, Kansas! Nestled in the heart of this vibrant college town, University Digs offers the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and community for students and local residents alike.

Step into a world of modern living with our thoughtfully designed studio, one-bedroom, and two-bedroom apartments. Each unit boasts sleek granite countertops and gleaming stainless steel appliances, creating a stylish and functional living space. Say goodbye to laundromat runs with in-unit washers and dryers, giving you more time to focus on what matters most.

At University Digs, we understand that your home extends beyond your front door. That's why we've created an array of amenities to keep you active, social, and entertained. Dive into our refreshing pool on hot summer days, or challenge your friends to a game of sand volleyball. Pickleball enthusiasts will love our dedicated courts, while those seeking a more leisurely activity can enjoy a game of bocce ball. And that's just the beginning there's always something happening at University Digs!
